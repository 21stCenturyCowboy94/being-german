"Strive not to be an individual of success, but an individual of value." - Albert Einstein

Hallo my friends. I'm sure you know about people like Albert Einstein, Ludwig van Beethoven, Johann Sebastain Bach, etc. Well if you have heard of them, let me tell you a fact: They're all from Germany!


In this page I will be exploring a number of Germans who made history, and a description about them and the lives.


The majority of the figures shown in this are deceased, but there are a few of them that are still living

1. Ludwig van Beethoven


Ludwig van Beethoven was a composer, born in Bonn, North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany in 1770. He is often considered to be one of the greatest composers of classical music in history.

2. Werner von Siemens


Werner von Siemens was a German inventor and industrialist. He was best known for creating the company which features his last name (Siemens Inc.), which is a company that makes technology and machinery around the world.

Catherine the Great


Catherine The Great was a German Princess who became Empress of Russia. Although she is known for her time as being Queen of Russia, she was still German and should be mentioned. Catherine was born in Stettin, Prussia (now Szczecin, Pomerania, Poland) in 1729.
